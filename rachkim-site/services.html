Our Services

From a weekly home clean to a full commercial contract — we have a service for every space and every need.

01

Residential Cleaning

Your home deserves more than a quick wipe-down. We clean with care, consistency, and attention to every corner — leaving your space genuinely spotless.

Available as a once-off clean, weekly, bi-weekly or monthly service — entirely on your schedule.

  • Regular weekly and bi-weekly cleaning
  • Deep cleaning and spring cleans
  • Kitchen and bathroom sanitising
  • Dusting, vacuuming, mopping throughout
  • Bedroom and living area cleaning
02

Commercial Cleaning

A clean workspace boosts productivity and leaves the right impression on clients and staff. We offer flexible commercial cleaning contracts for businesses of all sizes.

After-hours and weekend slots available so we never disrupt your operations.

  • Office and boardroom cleaning
  • Retail and showroom cleaning
  • After-hours and weekend slots
  • Regular maintenance contracts
  • Kitchenette and bathroom sanitising
03

Pre- & Post-Occupational Cleaning

Moving in or out of a property? We ensure your new space is spotless before you settle in, or your old one is left in perfect condition after you leave.

  • Full pre-move-in deep clean
  • Post-move-out cleaning for landlords and tenants
  • Cupboards, appliances and fixtures cleaned
  • Windows, tracks and sills
  • Available on short notice
04

Carpet, Couch & Laundry

Using specialist equipment we deep-clean carpets, rugs and upholstery — removing stains, odours and built-up dirt that regular vacuuming cannot reach.

  • Carpet steam and dry cleaning
  • Couch and upholstery deep clean
  • Rug and curtain cleaning
  • Laundry and ironing services
  • Mattress cleaning
05

Window & Pest Control

Crystal-clear windows and a pest-free home — two services that make a huge difference to how your space looks and feels.

  • Interior and exterior window cleaning
  • Window tracks and frames
  • Insect and rodent pest control
  • Safe, effective treatments
  • Preventative pest management
06

Event & Post-Renovation Cleanup

After a party, gathering, or construction project — we step in and restore your space to perfection, no matter the scale of the mess.

  • Post-party and event cleanup
  • Post-renovation dust and debris removal
  • Large-scale commercial cleanup
  • Same-day and next-day availability
  • Full waste removal included
