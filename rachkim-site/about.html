Who We Are

A cleaning company based in Johannesburg.

Rachkim Cleaning Services is a professional cleaning company based in Johannesburg, South Africa. We were founded with a simple mission: to bring a genuinely high standard of cleanliness to homes and businesses across Johannesburg.

We offer a wide range of services including residential and commercial cleaning, pre- and post-occupational cleaning, carpet and couch cleaning, laundry, window cleaning, pest control, and event or post-renovation cleanup.

We are known for our reliability, our customer-first approach, and for going the extra mile to ensure every client is genuinely satisfied. Quality drives our success — and your satisfaction is always our standard.

Business Hours: Monday to Saturday 07:00 – 18:00 | Sunday by arrangement
